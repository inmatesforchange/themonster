WHAT WE REQUIRE FROM OUR PROGRAM PARTICIPANTS | VOUCHER DISBURSEMENTS
We require Job Seekers who enroll in our program to complete it within 7 days, using only online methods and other resources we provide. Many Employers of Choice use online Learning Management Systems to train their workforce. [Check out LinkedIn's 2018 Workplace Learning Report.] Our training program helps Job Seekers transition into this trending method of learning. We also communicate with them throughout their training to ensure timely and professional responses to our communications, exams, and assessments. Students must comply with our business and training standards. Failure to comply will result in them being excluded from our Job Placement Assistance Program. We make sure your Certificate of Excellence Vouchers are given only to those who are willing to do what it takes to be among the best.

GAPS IN EMPLOYMENT HISTORY FOR THE FORMERLY INCARCERATED - DEBUNKING THE MYTH
The Illinois state prison system requires inmates to acquire and maintain employment during their entire period of incarceration. It is not unusual for inmates to contribute to the overall profitability of the prison system, as well as assist with achieving and maintaining Operational Excellence for the Warden. Therefore, there aren't any employment gaps from the time the person is incarcerated until he is released from the prison system. In fact, it is very likely that many learned new skills that are easily transferable to your organization.

It is unlikely that people with criminal records, who are also qualified to execute the duties of the positions you're seeking to fill, will return to a life of crime if allowed to earn a living with an Employer of Choice.

If you have job opportunities for qualified graduates of our training program, then please send complete job descriptions, requirements, and compensation packages to mjones@spiralgroup.org. We will refer only our best to you. Along with job placement assistance, we provide Job Retention services to our graduates.
