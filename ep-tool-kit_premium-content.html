WHAT IS EMPLOYMENT READINESS?

Employment readiness is defined as being able to find, acquire, and keep an appropriate job, as well as to be able to manage transitions to new jobs as needed. Becoming employment ready involves being self-sufficient and capable of knowing what type of work suits you. Having the skills and education for the work you want, as well as the skills to find work. It also involves the seemingly endless task of career management and being able to manage future work-life changes. Having solid Soft Skills can assist with managing challenges and incline you to perform well.

If you truly want success along your career path, then you are willing to take responsibility for creating it. We are grateful that you chose us as your Training Services Provider. Our main goal is to help Job Seekers improve their organizational, decision-making, customer service, and soft skills. Upon successful completion of this program, participants are deemed JOB READY and receive a Certificate of Excellence that can be used alongside their other self-marketing materials. Good luck with your training, and we look forward to assisting you with acquiring gainful employment soon.
