SERVSAFE® FOOD HANDLER CERTIFICATE COURSE

OVERVIEW
In this class, food service workers learn how to properly prepare and serve food. The information imparted to food service workers in this program is based on the FDA food code, and is meant to provide basic guidelines for safe food handling.

Although this course has no official prerequisites and may be completed by any person seeking knowledge of safe food handling procedures, it is required for food service workers employed in restaurants, bars, nursing homes, licensed day care facilities, hospitals, schools, and long-term care facilities.

COST PER PERSON
The cost per person for an onsite class at your location is $15. This price includes:
Current study materials delivered via email prior to the class to help students prepare for the assessment.
A Certified ServSafe® Food Safety Instructor to conduct the classroom instruction, followed by the assessment.

OTHER IMPORTANT INFORMATION
Classes are available for groups of 3 or more and conducted at your location. One-to-one classes are also available for individuals.
On the day of class, students will need to bring a current state issued picture ID.
The assessment is taken online for immediate results. Same day certificates are available with a passing score of 75% or higher.
Classes are available in English, Spanish, and Chinese.
Computers or tablets must be provided.
The ServSafe® Food Handler Certificate is valid for 3 years in the state of Illinois.

If you took an Online Course and Assessment, your results will be available immediately after passing your assessment. You can print your certificate by selecting the "Print My Certificate" option under the Student section of www.ServSafe.com/ss/foodhandler.

HOW DOES THE SERVSAFE FOOD HANDLER CERTIFICATE DIFFER FROM THE SERVSAFE® FOOD PROTECTION MANAGER CERTIFICATION?
The ServSafe® Food Handler Certificate verifies basic food safety knowledge and is for individuals in food handler employee-level positions. Upon successful completion of the ANSI ASTM 2659 accredited Food Handler course and 40-question assessment, the employee will receive a Certificate of Achievement from the National Restaurant Association that can be printed from a secure section of the ServSafe® website.

The ServSafe® Food Protection Manager Certification verifies that a manager, or person-in-charge, has sufficient food safety knowledge to protect the public from foodborne illness. Individuals that successfully pass the exam will receive a ServSafe® Food Protection Manager Certification. The ServSafe® Food Protection Manager Certification is accredited by the American National Standards Institute (ANSI) under the Conference for Food Protection Standards.

SERVSAFE® FSSMC COURSE & EXAM

OVERVIEW
Receive your Food Service Sanitation Manager Certification (FSSMC) with Spiral Group, Inc., and trust ServSafe—the only food safety program with roots in the foodservice industry to help you protect customer health, improve employee performance, and preserve your business reputation.
