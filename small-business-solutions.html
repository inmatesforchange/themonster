MOCK HEALTH INSPECTIONS

OVERVIEW
Every organization in the food industry is subject to regular health inspections conducted by local government health departments. These inspections are designed to measure a food establishment's sanitation. If conditions are found to be unsanitary, they can pose a serious health risk to potential customers.

DISCOVER THE SPIRAL GROUP, INC. DIFFERENCE
We prepare you for an official City of Chicago Health Department Inspection. We work closely with our clients to ensure food safety by reviewing your last health inspection report and developing a strategy that will increase the possibility of your food establishment receiving a higher grade. During your Mock Health Inspection, we spend time training your food service workers on how to consistently prevent some of the most common health code violations. Click here to see videos of some of our favorite eateries facing major Health Code Violations.

FOOD SAFETY MANAGEMENT PROGRAM
Included with the Mock Health Inspection is a customized Food Safety Management Program. We create and assist with the implementation of the program for your restaurant based on your operations. We begin by identifying biological, chemical, and physical hazards associated with facility construction, equipment, employees, training, food, and supplies. We observe personnel hygiene; equipment and facility cleaning, sanitizing, and maintenance; and receiving and storage. Your Food Safety Management Program will contain procedures that either prevent, eliminate, or reduce the risk of hazards.

THE BENEFITS FROM ADHERING TO A CURRENT FOOD SAFETY MANAGEMENT PROGRAM
You'll know how to identify and eliminate chemical, biological, and physical contaminants in the food products offered to your consumers, as well as determine which hazards are significant health risks.
You'll be able to provide an adequate level of protection for your customers.
You can train and supervise food service workers to perform controls that strive for zero food safety hazards and foodborne illness outbreaks.

CLEANING & MAINTENANCE PROGRAM
Food service establishments must adhere to a proper cleaning and maintenance schedule to prevent conditions that can lead to the entry and nesting of pests. We assist with the development and implementation of a comprehensive Cleaning & Maintenance Program based on your food service establishment's hours of operation and peak/non-peak periods.
