MYSTERY SHOP PROGRAM

OVERVIEW
A Mystery Shop Program allows you to evaluate how your company delivers its products and services. You’ve developed a set of guidelines that drives operational excellence, but you can’t be there 24 hours a day to ensure standards are being met by your workforce. Let us anonymously visit or call your locations to answer questionnaires designed to provide you with an unbiased view of your operations. We observe the following:
Wait and service times.
Impact of team training.
Sales force effectiveness.
Customer refund experiences.
The on-site customer experience.
Quality of products and services.
Customer greetings, departures, and invitations to return.
Upselling of products and services.
Selection and availability.
Workforce appearance, friendliness, and engagement.
Cleanliness, functionality, and condition of restrooms.
General appearance of locations - interior and exterior.
