Thank you for taking the time to request a Free Phone Consultation with one of our Business Consultants.
We are very excited and delighted that you are considering Spiral Group, Inc. as your Small Business Solutions Provider.

A Business Operations Consultant can be an invaluable resource for small business owners. Therefore, we've provided some tips that should help you get the most from your Free Phone Consultation.

Be Prepared.
Know what you want to accomplish during your consultation. Have specific issues you'd like to discuss and specific questions you'd like to ask. Be ready to present any facts or figures that would shed light on your current business operations, and to provide follow-up information if your Business Consultant needs more details in order to provide effective responses to your questions.

Being prepared with an objective for this consultation will decrease the likelihood of time being spent on issues that don't really need to be discussed during the free consultation. As a result, we will be more inclined to get to the core of your most important business concerns.

Peruse the Contents of Our Web Pages.
If you haven't done so already, we suggest perusing the contents of the following web pages so that you'll have a clear understanding of the products and services we provide: Small Business Solutions | HR Compliance & Risk Mitigation Tools | ServSafe® Courses & Exams.

Leave Time for Your Consultant to Make Comments & Ask Questions.
Although you may have a clear objective in mind, your Business Consultant may feel the need to address certain items, as they pertain to achieving and maintaining operational excellence, during the consultation.

Take Notes.
Take notes on any overarching themes that come out of the conversation. Don't forget to jot down your own ideas and input.

Create Action Items.
It is suggested that you close the consultation with one clear objective in mind, to Create Action Items. This will allow you to begin the process of putting to use what you discover during the consultation. These action items should be specific and have solid deadlines.
