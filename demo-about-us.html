SPIRAL GROUP, INC.

Spiral Group, Inc. is a not for profit organization located in Chicago, Illinois. We offer affordable BUSINESS SOLUTIONS to locally under-served Small Businesses. We're committed to assisting this population of entrepreneurs by providing the BUSINESS SOLUTIONS and workforce training tools they need to ensure operational excellence, at affordable rates.

We leverage our skills and years of corporate experience to help our clients build and maintain reliable companies that can support the needs of their markets. We work to refine operational infrastructure by establishing lean systems & processes, and the outsourcing of non-core services. As a result, our clients enjoy reduced operating costs and improved workforce productivity.
