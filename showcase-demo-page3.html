S-M-P

MOCK HEALTH INSPECTION

Every organization in the food industry is subject to regular public health inspections conducted by local government health departments. These inspections are designed to measure a food establishment's level of sanitation. If conditions are found to be unsanitary, they can pose serious health risks to your customers. We have the knowledge, skill-set, and credentials to prepare you for an official City of Chicago Health Department Inspection.

Why Choose SpiralGroup?

We work closely with you to ensure food safety, while reviewing your last health inspection report. We develop systems that increase your chances of receiving a higher grade. During the inspection, we train your food service workers on how to prevent health code violations. See videos of some of our favorite eateries facing major Health Code Violations.
