MOCK HEALTH INSPECTION

Every organization in the food industry is subject to regular public health inspections conducted by local government health departments. These inspections are designed to measure a food establishment's level of sanitation. If conditions are found to be unsanitary, they can pose serious health risks to your customers.

We help you prepare for an official City of Chicago Health Department Inspection.
