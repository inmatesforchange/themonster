WHAT IS EMPLOYMENT READINESS?

Employment Readiness is defined as being able to find and keep an appropriate job, and being capable of managing transitions to new jobs. Being Employment Ready only means that you are self-sufficient, know the kind of work that suits you, and make realistic choices along your career-path. It means knowing that if you really want career success, then you are willing to take responsibility for creating it.

We are grateful that you chose us as your Training Services Provider. Our goal is to help you improve your organizational, decision-making, customer service, and soft skills. When you complete this program, you can purchase a Certificate of Excellence for only $39.99 that can be used alongside your other self-marketing materials. Good luck with your training.
