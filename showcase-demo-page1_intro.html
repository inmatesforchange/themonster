THE RETURNING CITIZEN

Michelle Jones is the Founder and CEO of Spiral Group, Inc. She began developing the company after serving a 2.5 year sentence at the Logan Correctional facility in 2014. She initiated the task of rebuilding her life by utilizing her skills and corporate experience in an effort to make a useful and practical contribution to the Small Business Community.

In April 2016, she launched this website. Michelle began by developing a free, online Employment Preparation Training program for job seekers with criminal histories — those who are willing to commit to positive change. The NFP's goal is to assist returning citizens in achieving self-sufficiency, while reducing an inclination toward recidivism.

MORE ABOUT SPIRAL GROUP, INC.

The company is dedicated to helping job seekers with criminal histories overcome many obstacles to employment by improving their organizational, time-management, decision-making, interviewing, and soft skills.

Along her career path, Michelle has worked with the following organizations: Subway, Portillo's, Quiznos, Dock's Great Fish, Burger King, Chili's, Target, and Jeepers. She has over 20 years' experience in the hospitality and food-service industries as an hourly worker, cashier supervisor, assistant manager, general manager, trainer, training manager, and franchise business operations consultant.

Michelle opened her first business, RMRC (Restaurant Materials Resource Center), in 1999 where she designed business documents, labels, and posters for the food-service industry.

She is a Certified ServSafe® Instructor and Registered ServSafe® Examination Proctor. Michelle has been a web developer since 1999, and an ordained minister since 2007.
