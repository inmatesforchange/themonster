SPIRAL GROUP, INC.

Spiral Group, Inc. is a not for profit organization located in Chicago, Illinois. We offer affordable business solutions to locally underserved Small Businesses. We're committed to assisting this population of entrepreneurs by providing the tools they need to ensure operational excellence, at affordable rates.

We leverage our skills and corporate experience to help clients build reliable companies that can support the needs of their markets. We work to refine operational infrastructure by establishing lean systems and processes, as well as encourage the outsourcing of non-core services. As a result, entrepreneurs can enjoy reduced operating costs and improved workforce productivity.

Your Small Business has greater growth potential than you probably realize. Many consumers value Small Businesses that can professionally and consistently meet their specialized needs. This is a great time for entrepreneurs to begin taking advantage of profitable opportunities by spending time making strategic decisions on how to further develop their companies and increasing brand awareness.

THE RETURNING CITIZEN

Michelle Jones is the Founder and CEO of Spiral Group, Inc. She began developing the company after serving a 2.5 year sentence at the Logan Correctional facility in 2014. She initiated the task of rebuilding her life by utilizing her skills and corporate experience in an effort to make a useful and practical contribution to the Small Business Community. In April 2016, she launched this website.

Michelle began by developing a free, online Employment Preparation Training program for job seekers with criminal histories — those who are willing to commit to positive change. The NFP's goal is to assist returning citizens in achieving self-sufficiency, while reducing an inclination toward recidivism. The company is dedicated to helping this population of job seekers overcome many obstacles to employment by improving their organizational, time-management, decision-making, interviewing, and soft skills.

Along her career path, Michelle has worked with the following organizations: Subway, Portillo's, Quiznos, Dock's Great Fish, Burger King, Chili's, Target, and Jeepers. She opened her first business, RMRC (Restaurant Materials Resource Center), in 1999 where she designed business documents, labels, and posters for the food-service industry.

She is a Certified ServSafe® Instructor and Registered ServSafe® Examination Proctor. Michelle has over 20 years' experience in the hospitality and food-service industries as an hourly worker, cashier supervisor, assistant manager, general manager, trainer, training manager, and franchise business operations consultant. Michelle is a web developer, attaining webmaster status in 1999. She is also an ordained minister, receiving her ordination on April 19, 2007.
